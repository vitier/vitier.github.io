Research

Digital Forensics interest is growing, both in the academic world and in society ("fake news", "deep fakes", ...). Image falsifications aim to change the semantics of an image (and therefore the interpretation that can be done), and generally consist of endogenous or exogenous copy and paste (duplication, masking) or inpainting (deletion). Therefore my research interest lies between the fields of Digital Image Forensics, Watermarking, Machine Learning and Cryptography.
